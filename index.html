Key takeaways

Biggest strength

Peer connection was the runaway theme — GMs valued seeing colleagues as people, not just rankings. Random breakout groupings were specifically praised for sparking fresh perspectives.

Top opportunity

Venue (4.09) and food (3.78) are the two weakest scores. Multiple respondents suggested a location change and better catering. A 2-day format was the most-requested structural change.

Content demand

P&L depth is far and away the #1 topic request — respondents want more time, more detail, and a true class format rather than a review. Power BI, Win Score, and labor/scheduling also surfaced repeatedly.

Discussion management

Multiple respondents flagged that some participants dominated discussions. Consider structured facilitation or timed contributions to keep sessions equitable and on track.

Recognition & awards

The awards ceremony was a top-cited favorite. Consider expanding award categories (most improvement, best yearly avg) to recognize more attendees meaningfully.

Plus-one & afterparty

21 of 36 said bringing a plus-one enhanced their experience. Of those who attended the afterparty, 28 of 29 reported connecting with peers — only 1 said no.
